Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 tbsp salt
1 tbsp pepper
1 tbsp coconut oil

Instructions:
1. Get a frying pan and apply coconut oil to pan.
2. Turn on stove to medium heat to heat up the oil.
3. Crack the eggs into a small cup or bowl.
4. Add the salt and pepper to the cup and mix for a few seconds.
5. Pour egg mixture into the pan.
6. Cook eggs for a few minutes and stir occasionally until eggs turn solid.
7. Transfer scrambled eggs onto a plate and enjoy!
